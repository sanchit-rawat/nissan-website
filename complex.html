The Legacy of Nissan

FROM SKYLINE TO SUPERCAR

The History

1933

Jidosha-Seizo Kabushiki-Kaisha

The company that would become Nissan is established in Yokohama, Japan.

1969

Birth of the Z & GT-R

The legendary Fairlady Z (Datsun 240Z) and the first-generation Skyline GT-R (PGC10 "Hakosuka") are introduced.

1989

The Godzilla Era Begins

Nissan unleashes the Skyline GT-R R32, dominating Group A racing and earning the nickname "Godzilla."

2007

The Supercar Killer

The R35 GT-R launches independently from the Skyline nameplate, shattering Nürburgring records.
