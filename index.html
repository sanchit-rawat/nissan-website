Decades of Dominance

1933

The Foundation

Jidosha-Seizo Kabushiki-Kaisha is established in Yokohama, marking the birth of the brand that would become Nissan.

1969

Birth of the Legends

The automotive world is changed forever with the release of the Fairlady Z (240Z) and the Hakosuka Skyline GT-R.

1989

Godzilla Awakens

The R32 Skyline GT-R launches, utterly dominating Group A racing and cementing the GT-R legacy.

2007

The Supercar Killer

Nissan releases the R35 GT-R. Dropping the Skyline name, it becomes a standalone engineering marvel.

2024

The Modern Era

Honoring its roots with the new Nissan Z, while pushing the boundaries of EV performance globally.
